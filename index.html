I'm a TDD coach

I had the chance to learn from one of the best and since then consider untested code as worthless in the log-term. Writing tests makes you sleep better and completely removes those moments of "I'm afraid of touching the code". As a side effect following the rules of TDD will heavily influence the way you code in a good sense. Since 2012 I offer TDD coaching as part of my job at Uxebu.
